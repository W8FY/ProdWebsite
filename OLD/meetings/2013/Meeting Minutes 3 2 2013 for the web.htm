March 2, 2013

 

 

The regular meeting of the Van Wert Amateur Radio Club was called to order by the Vice President Dave Taylor at 8:00 pm local time on March 2, 2013, with 10 voting members and 1 associate member present. The minutes from the February 16th, 2013 meeting was read and approved with corrections.  

 

The Treasurer reported a cash balance on hand.  Motion to accept the report by Larry Swaney, 2nd by Duane Poling.  Motion passed and report filed for audit.

 

COMMITTEE / OFFICERS REPORTS:

None

 

ANNOUNCEMENTS:

President: Informed the club that Rick McCoy, EMA Director will hold a “severe weather awareness and spotter course” on Wednesday, March 20, 2013 at the Marsh Foundation Auditorium. Time is 7:00 pm – 9:00 pm.

 

OLD BUSINESS: 

Michelle Thomas: Reported that spring banquet was being finalized. It will be held at the American Legion on May 4th. The entertainment will be on weather folklore.
