## 🛰️ Frequency Info

- **Frequency:** 444.875 MHz  
- **Offset:** +5.000 MHz  
- **PL Tone:** 118.8 Hz  
- **Mode:** FM

## 📡 Usage

This UHF repeater serves as:

- A backup communications repeater
- A coverage test system during events
- An auxiliary option when VHF traffic is high
